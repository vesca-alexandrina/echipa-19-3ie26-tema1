About CapeGo

Our Story

CapeGo was founded in 2018 by a group of Cape Town locals who believed that the best travel experiences come from genuine connections. Tired of cookie-cutter tours that rushed through landmarks, we set out to create something different — immersive journeys that reveal the soul of the Mother City.

From our first sunset hike up Lion's Head to the thousands of travellers we've guided since, our passion for Cape Town has only grown deeper. Today, CapeGo is trusted by visitors from over 40 countries who come seeking adventure, culture, and memories that last a lifetime.
